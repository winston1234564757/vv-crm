# Product

## Register

product

## Users

The entire business runs through one person — the owner, who also works as manager and repair master. The tool must serve all three hats without friction. In larger scenarios, employees (sales staff, repair technicians) use the same interface. Context: behind a counter or in a workshop, on desktop, during active customer interactions.

## Product Purpose

VV CRM is an internal system for selling electronics (phones, laptops, accessories) and managing repair jobs. It replaces scattered notes, spreadsheets, and memory. Success looks like: a repair customer gets a tracking link, stock is always accurate, and the daily snapshot of sales and repairs is visible in one glance.

## Brand Personality

Warm, human, simple. This is not a cold enterprise tool — it's a craftsperson's companion. Think workshop bench, not boardroom. Approachable, direct, quietly confident. Ukrainian context shapes the tone: pragmatic, no corporate BS.

## Anti-references

- NOT a 1С-style heavy ERP with dense grids and Soviet UX
- NOT a boring Bootstrap admin panel with grey sidebar and three columns of tables
- NOT hyped SaaS — no gradient overload, no glassmorphism, no animations competing for attention
- NOT a generic "modern startup" landing page aesthetic

## Design Principles

1. **One tool, every hat** — same product serves owner, manager, and master. Personalize by role without separate apps.
2. **Warm clarity** — human-first, not machine-first. Data is approachable, not intimidating. The interface breathes.
3. **Simple, not simplistic** — powerful features live behind clean surfaces. Reduce cognitive load without losing capability.
4. **Anti-cliché** — reject every CRM default. No hero-metric template, no identical card grids, no side-stripe accents.
5. **Craft over hype** — every pixel earns its place. Motion has purpose. Color has reason. Silence is a design tool.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Basic WCAG AA compliance for contrast and keyboard navigation. No special requirements beyond standard web accessibility.
